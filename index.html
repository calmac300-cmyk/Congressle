You will be shown a series of historic votes cast by a single member of Congress. Use the clues to deduce who they are.

How to Play

  1. You'll see 3 historic votes cast by today's mystery legislator.
  2. Type a name to search and submit your guess.
  3. Each guess reveals clues — state, region, party, era, and votes.
  4. A new vote is revealed with each wrong guess.
  5. You have 6 guesses to identify the legislator.
