  4. A new vote is revealed with each wrong guess.
  5. You have 6 guesses to identify the legislator.

How to Play

Each day a mystery member of Congress is chosen. You are shown a selection of their historic votes and must identify who they are in 6 guesses.

Feedback Tiles

WV Green — exact match
NE Yellow — same region, wrong state · or era within 8 years
CA Grey — no match

Categories

  • State — the state they represented
  • Region — Census region (Northeast / South / Midwest / West)
  • Party — their party affiliation
  • Chamber — Senate or House
  • Era — their years in Congress. An arrow (↑ later / ↓ earlier) shows which direction to guess
  • Votes — one tile per visible vote. Green = same vote, Yellow = both present but opposite sides, Grey = not in office or absent

Vote Clues

You start with 3 votes. A new vote is revealed with each wrong guess. Hover over any vote name to read a description of what the vote was about.

Difficulty

Senate puzzles feature well-known long-serving senators. House puzzles draw from a larger pool and district geography plays a bigger role.

About

The Congressional Record is a daily deduction game built on historic U.S. roll-call vote data from Voteview (UCLA) and district boundaries from cdmaps.

A new puzzle is available each day at midnight UTC.
